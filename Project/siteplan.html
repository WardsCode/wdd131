Project Site Plan

Site Name

Comic House

This name represents a comic page focused on delivering webcomics to an audience around the world

Domain availability: The-House-Of-Comic.com

Site Purpose

This page will provide multiple stories created by the best artist around the world. You will be able to read these stories from your computer or celphone. It lists the comics by genre and in alphabetical order, it also provide summaries about them so you can easily find the ones you preffer.
